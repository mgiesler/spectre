Detailed Description

Linear map from \(\xi \in [A, B]\rightarrow x \in [a, b]\). The formula for the mapping is...

\[ x = \frac{b}{B-A} (\xi-A) +\frac{a}{B-A}(B-\xi) \]

\[ \xi =\frac{B}{b-a} (x-a) +\frac{A}{b-a}(b-x) \]
