template<size_t Dim>
double Basis::lgl::definite_integral ( const Scalar< DataVector > &  integrand,
const Index< Dim > &  extents 
)
noexcept

Compute the definite integral of a grid-function over a manifold.

The integral is computed on the reference element by multiplying the DataVector with the Basis::lgl integration weights in that dimension.

Requires: number of points in integrand and extents are equal.

Parameters
integrandthe grid function to integrate.
extentsthe extents of the manifold on which integrand is located.

Returns: the definite integral of integrand on the manifold.

References ASSERT, Index< Dim >::product(), DataVector::size(), and Index< Dim >::slice_away().
