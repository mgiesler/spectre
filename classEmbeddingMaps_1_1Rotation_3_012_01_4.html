Detailed Description

template<>
class EmbeddingMaps::Rotation< 2 >

Spatial rotation in two dimensions. Let \((R,\Phi)\) be the polar coordinates associated with \((\xi,\eta)\). Let \((r,\phi)\) be the polar coordinates associated with \((x,y)\). Applies the spatial rotation \(\phi = \Phi + \alpha\).

The formula for the mapping is:

\begin{eqnarray*} x &=& \xi \cos \alpha - \eta \sin \alpha \\ y &=& \xi \sin \alpha + \eta \cos \alpha \end{eqnarray*}
